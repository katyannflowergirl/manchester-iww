Hospitality Workers

Low pay? Missing Wages or tips? Harrassed at work?

Manchester Hospitality Workers is a group of hospitality workers and supporters in Greater Manchester building a union for all workers in bars, cafes, pubs, takeaways and restaurants. Affiliated to Manchester IWW.

If you work in hospitality, and want to find out more, get in touch!

Across the world, hospitality workers are organising and fighting back against low pay, harassment and wage theft:

  • Pizza Hut Workers Union
  • Starbucks Workers Union
  • Norwich Bartenders and Hospitality Workers Union
  • Jimmy Johns Workers Union

Join the IWW today – a fighting union for all workers.
