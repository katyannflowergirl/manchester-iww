Meetings

We currently hold meetings around the start of each month – usually in central Manchester.

For details of the next meeting and minutes from previous meetings, please subscribe to the mailing list and check out the archives.

Contact

For membership, press and other enquiries please send a message to manchester@iww.org.uk, or give us a ring on 07961 933 632.

If you are an IWW member in the Greater Manchester area, the best way to stay in touch is to join our e-mail list: go to the list information page and enter your details to request to join the list, and one of our members will approve your request. You will then be able to view previous messages on the "Archive" page.
